# sp_academy/library/archive — The Archive

## Mode: Archive

A place to respectfully forget. This space holds finished work, retired material, and snapshots. It is visitable, not active.

## Conventions

**Archiving is not deletion.** Material here is honored, not discarded. It served its purpose.

**Archived items should be findable.** Use clear naming so future-you can locate things if needed.

**The archive does not require maintenance.** Neglect is acceptable — this is a resting place.

## Shell Commands

| Command | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| `archive` | Go to archive root |

## Working Here

- Visit when you need to remember, not to resume
- If something in the archive wants to live again, move it back to its appropriate space
- Completed commissions arrive here from `workshop/commissions/`
- Dissolved threads may leave traces here
- When in doubt about whether to delete or archive, archive
