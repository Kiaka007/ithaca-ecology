> This is a quick reference that tracks rule-of-thumb advice for gardening

## Above-ground Crops
- Should be planted when the moon is waxing
- new moons are good for leafy greens
- first-quarters are good for externally bearing fruit like tomatoes and beans

## Root Crops
- Should be planted when the moon is waning
- They should be planted in the first half
- During the last half no planting should be done, instead improve the soil via mulching, weeding, composting etc.
-
## Harvesting
- Harvest in the waning phases
-
## Fertilizing
- Fertilize during the waning phases (this does not apply to when you first plant)
-
## Pruning
- To discourage growth prune during waxing phases
- To encourage growth prune during the waning phases
