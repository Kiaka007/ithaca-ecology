# Arcane Scholar — Daily Prompt Constellations

These prompts align **mode of work** with **energy of the day**.
They are invitations, not instructions.
Use one prompt at a time.

---

## I. Research-Heavy Day  
**Goal:** Seeing (not deciding)

### Early Morning — WONDER
- What question is still quietly open from yesterday?
- What felt confusing but alive?
- What am I curious about without knowing why?
- What am I *not* trying to resolve today?

### Late Morning — PLAY
- What happens if I exaggerate this?
- What happens if I remove the thing I assume matters?
- What’s the smallest variation I could try?
- What am I noticing emotionally, not analytically?

### Early Afternoon — MOVE
- What am I noticing without interpretation?
- What patterns show up when I’m not looking directly?
- What feels stuck in my body vs my head?

### Late Afternoon — DISTILL
- What did I actually observe?
- What surprised me?
- What failed in an interesting way?
- What feels important but still unclear?

### Evening — REFLECT
- What new question emerged?
- What assumption feels weaker now?
- What would be worth exploring tomorrow?

---

## II. Project-Heavy Day  
**Goal:** Deciding (not discovering)

### Early Morning — ANCHOR
- What decision am I actually facing?
- What outcome matters *today*?
- What am I willing to leave unresolved?

### Late Morning — EVALUATE
- Does this serve the project’s purpose?
- What trade-off am I making by choosing this?
- What am I excluding on purpose?

### Early Afternoon — TEST
- Does this decision feel coherent?
- Where does it feel brittle?
- What resistance am I noticing?

### Late Afternoon — FORMALIZE
- What did I decide today?
- Why this and not the alternatives?
- What assumptions does this lock in?

### Evening — CLOSE
- What uncertainty did I accept?
- What pressure did I release?
- Does the project feel clearer than this morning?

---

## III. Writing-Heavy Day  
**Goal:** Communicating & Synthesizing

### Early Morning — ORIENT
- What am I actually trying to say?
- Who is this for?
- What do I care about here?

### Late Morning — DRAFT
- What’s the through-line?
- Where does the argument feel thin?
- What tension is doing the work?

### Early Afternoon — SENSE
- What parts feel alive?
- What parts feel forced?
- Where do I lose interest?

### Late Afternoon — SHAPE
- What needs clarification vs removal?
- Where am I hiding behind abstraction?
- How can I say this more simply?

### Evening — RELEASE
- What did this writing help me understand?
- What remains unsaid?
- Did I say something true today?

---

## Use Notes
- Choose **one mode** per day
- Choose **one time-appropriate prompt**
- Stop when energy shifts
