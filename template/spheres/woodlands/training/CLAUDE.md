# sp_woodlands/training — Training

## Mode: Balance

The Training space holds activities that tend the whole self across four elemental modes. These are not fitness goals or self-improvement projects. They are practices of balance — noticing where energy flows and where it has been absent.

## The Four Elements

The elements are integrated and mutually balancing:

- **⛰️ Earth** balances **🌀 Wind** — When the mind runs too fast or too long, the body grounds it. Physical practice (movement, hands in soil, embodied craft) returns you to the present.

- **🌀 Wind** balances **⛰️ Earth** — When you are heavy or stuck, mental clarity lifts. Contemplative practice, reading, and reflection open space.

- **🌊 Water** balances **🔥 Fire** — When creative fire burns too intensely or in isolation, relational connection softens and sustains it. Care, conversation, and presence with others.

- **🔥 Fire** balances **🌊 Water** — When relational life becomes all-consuming or emotionally heavy, creative expression transforms and releases. Making something shifts the current.

## What Lives Here

- **manuals/** (optional) — Training activity entries as `.md` files with yaml frontmatter, organized however suits you (by season, by elemental path, or by activity type).

## Norms

- Approach training as tending, not tracking. The goal is noticing balance, not accumulating completions.
- Seasonal training aligns with natural energy: Yule invites rest and wind; Beltane calls for earth and fire; Mabon asks for water and release.
- No element is more important than another. All four are always present to some degree.
