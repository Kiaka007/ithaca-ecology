# sp_woodlands/rituals — Rituals

## Mode: Attune

The Rituals space holds spiritual activities aligned to the Wheel of the Year and the lunar cycle. These are practices of attunement — marking the turning of seasons, honoring natural thresholds, and cultivating presence.

## The Wheel of the Year

Eight seasonal moments, each with its own character:

| Sabbat | Approx. Date | Quality |
|---|---|---|
| **Yule** | Winter Solstice (~Dec 21) | Rest, return of light, deep reflection |
| **Imbolc** | ~Feb 1 | First stirrings, seeds of intention, creative quickening |
| **Ostara** | Spring Equinox (~Mar 21) | Balance, renewal, new growth |
| **Beltane** | ~May 1 | Vitality, connection, celebration of life |
| **Litha** | Summer Solstice (~Jun 21) | Peak energy, abundance, outward expression |
| **Lammas / Lughnasadh** | ~Aug 1 | First harvest, gratitude, beginning to turn inward |
| **Mabon** | Autumn Equinox (~Sep 21) | Balance again, deeper harvest, release |
| **Samhain** | ~Oct 31 | Thinning of veils, honoring endings, deep listening |

## What Lives Here

- **tomes/** (optional) — Ritual entries as `.md` files with yaml frontmatter, organized into three books:
  - `tomes/book-of-day/` — Ostara, Beltane, Litha, Lammas (active half of the year)
  - `tomes/book-of-night/` — Mabon, Samhain, Yule, Imbolc (inward half of the year)
  - `tomes/book-of-all-seasons/` — Year-round entries not tied to a specific season
- **offerings/** — active tally for the season that records activities taken that strengthen alignment within the four elements. Used by the `offer` and `balance` commands; created automatically on first use.

## Norms

- Rituals are suggestions aligned to the season, not obligations
- The practice is eclectic — activities may draw from any tradition: ceremony, gardening, art-making, or quiet observation
- The moon phase is also relevant: new moon for beginnings, full moon for completion and release
- Use `invoke` to check the current season and moon phase before engaging with this space
