# sp_town/townhall — The Townhall

## Mode: Relate & Record

The Townhall is where relationships with people and entities are tracked and tended. This is not a CRM — it is a record of meaningful connection.

## What Lives Here

- **address-book/** — Registry of trusted sources and people. See `address-book/CLAUDE.md`.
- **town-records/** (optional) — archive of past events, purse ledgers, and planning files that affect the construction of this ecology (ex. Claude plan files that get implemented)

## Norms

- Relationships here are ongoing — they require periodic attention, not just one-time logging
- Distinguish between contact types: direct relationships vs. entities worth tracking from a distance
- The address book is not exhaustive; it is curated. Quality of connection over quantity.
- When sourcing collaborators, reviewers, or venues — start here before searching broadly
