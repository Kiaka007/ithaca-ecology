# sp_town/market — The Market

## Mode: Trade & Publish

The Market is where finished or near-finished work meets the world. This is the outward-facing commerce layer — things ready to sell, share, or publish.

## What Lives Here

- **purse/** — ships as an empty folder. Houses the current season's tally for coins earned as well as the current season's coin goal, written automatically the first time you run `offer coin` or `purse_goal`. Read by `balance`.
- **my-shop/** (optional) — your own wares, portfolio, and public output. Ships with an `inventory/` subfolder — things ready to move but not yet public — as a starting point; rename or restructure freely.
- **other-shops/** (optional) — external markets, platforms, and venues worth tracking

## Norms

- Content here is outgoing, not incoming
- If something still needs significant work, it belongs in the Academy (workshop or drafts), not here
- Track the state of things: is this listed, live, sold, retired?
- The Market connects to the Guild — quests completed here may generate coin rewards
