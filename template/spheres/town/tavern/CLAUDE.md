# sp_town/tavern — The Tavern

## Mode: Gather & Consume

The Tavern is a place of intake — recipes, feeds, and curated information flows from the outside world. Nothing here is produced; it is received.

## What Lives Here

- **Recipes** — Cooking and craft recipes, organized however you prefer
- **RSS/feeds** — Curated information sources relevant to your work and interests

## Norms

- Content here is incoming, not outgoing
- Organize by use, not by source — what do you reach for, and when?
- Feeds should be curated, not comprehensive; the Tavern is a warm room, not an archive
